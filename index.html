Hi! I am Arshi; a passionate software developer from Calgary. I graduated from University of Victoria with a bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering in 2019. I am currently working at Jostle as a team lead and a developer.

My specialization in university was “Data Mining and Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning”. And lately, I’ve been exploring many other topics that excite me as a developer.

For example, my fascination with the use of technology in right-to-left languages like Arabic and by visually impaired users has inspired me to actively learn and enhance my skills in designing and developing software that are inclusive and accessible.

When I have some time to spare, I am working to publish more things on GitHub and add content on this website. So I invite you to explore my website, visit the Projects’ Page, and feel free to reach out and connect. I’d love to hear from you!
